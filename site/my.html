Frequently Asked Questions

What is drone light show?

A drone light show is a display of synchronized drones with LED lights that fly in patterns and formations to create a visually stunning spectacle, They are becoming increasingly popular for special events and are environmentally friendly and safer than traditional fireworks displays.

How much does a drone show cost?

The cost of a drone light show can vary depending on factors such as the number of drones, choreography complexity, location, and duration. Generally, a drone light show can cost tens of thousands to hundreds of thousands of dollars.

What is the show duration?

Drone light show durations can vary but typically last from 5 to 30 minutes, depending on event requirements and budget. It's best to discuss the desired duration with a professional drone light show provider.

Is it possible to fly above water?

Flying drones over water is possible, but there are additional risks and challenges. It's important to use waterproof drones, take necessary precautions, and follow regulations to ensure safe flights.

Is it possible to combine a drone show with live performance?

Combining drone light shows with live performances is possible and can create a visually stunning spectacle. However, it's crucial to work with experienced drone light show providers to ensure safety and success.

What is the maximum altitude that a drone swarm can reach?

The drones we use typically operate at altitudes ranging from 10 to 300 meters, and they can be visible up to a range of 5 kilometers.

What is the timeline for organizing a drone light show?

The timeline for organizing a drone light show can vary, taking several weeks to several months, depending on factors such as show size, complexity, and resources required.

What are the acceptable wind conditions for launching drones?

The acceptable wind conditions for launching drones depend on the specific drone model and the operator's skill level. Typically, drones can fly in winds up to 20 mph, but it's important to monitor wind speed and direction for safe operations.

What is the legality of flying drones over roads and highways?

Flying drones over roads and highways may not be legal in many countries due to safety concerns. It is important to follow local aviation regulations and obtain necessary permits or authorizations.

Is it possible for your drones to capture pictures or collect data?

Yes, our drones can capture images and collect data, depending on the model and equipment installed on them. The capabilities can be customized based on the needs of the event.

Is it possible to launch a drone show during rain and how will it affect the quality?

Rain can significantly affect the quality of a drone light show, as it impairs drone visibility, affects sensor accuracy, and poses a risk of water damage to electronic components. It's best to avoid flying drones in the rain, have backup plans.
